Why This Exists
Problem & Solution
A real-world challenge solved with cloud AI infrastructure.
⚠️
Problem Statement
Manual Image Identification
Manual image identification is slow, inconsistent, and error-prone at scale. Humans struggle to classify thousands of images accurately and quickly, and there's no structured way to store, search, or revisit those results.
✅
Solution
AI-Powered Cloud Pipeline
An AI-powered cloud application that automatically analyzes uploaded images, classifies content using Vision AI, stores results in cloud storage, and maintains a searchable scan history — all in real time with zero manual effort.
